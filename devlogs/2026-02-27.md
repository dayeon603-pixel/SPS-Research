# Dev Log — 2026-02-27: Jacobian Connection — Spectral Gap Idea

## What Happened
Connected SPS to the Jacobian of f. In the ε→0 limit, sensitivity is just the directional derivative along the perturbation direction — a JVP (Jacobian-vector product).

Sup over all directions in A_x gives the A_x-restricted operator norm of J_f(x).

## Spectral Gap
A model with high spectral gap has its most sensitive directions **geometrically orthogonal** to the semantic manifold. This is a desirable property — the model is sensitive to meaning-changing perturbations and invariant to meaning-preserving ones.

No existing metric captures this geometric relationship between the Jacobian spectrum and the semantic manifold.
