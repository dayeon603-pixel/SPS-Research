# Dev Log — 2026-03-09: Theorem 4 — Missing Chain Constraint

## What Happened
Theorem 4 (triangle inequality for chained transformations T2 ∘ T1) was missing a critical hypothesis: c(T2, T1·x) ≤ ε must hold — the second transformation must also be within the admissible radius when applied to the already-transformed input.

Without this, T2 ∘ T1 could push outside the ε-ball where Sens is defined, and the theorem doesn't apply.

## Fix
Added explicit hypothesis: both single steps must be within ε/2 to guarantee the composition stays within ε. This follows from A5 (closure under composition).

## Lesson
Composition theorems always need explicit domain constraints. The composed operation must stay in the domain where each component is valid.
